Croffle Recipe

Croffles are a mashup between croissants and waffles. They are basically made up of a croissant, pressed in a waffle maker to look like a waffle. Think waffle, but when you bite into it, you get several flakey layers and a nice, crispy exterior. Itâ€™s the mash up that nobody asked for, but that absolutely works!

Ingredients

  • Pre-made croissant dough*
  • Granulated sugar
  • Icing sugar
  • Maple syrup

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle maker to medium heat.
  2. Roll up croissant dough into crescent shapes.
  3. Roll croissant crescents in sugar.
  4. Cook in waffle maker until croffle is cooked through and GBD (golden brown & delicious).
  5. Serve with icing sugar sprinkled over top and a drizzle of maple syrup.
